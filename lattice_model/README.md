1. The lattic model simulator. To run the code:
python3 mc_cluster_two_chain_selective_allpossible.py <N> <eps_sol> <eps_bssg> [<bs1_index>,<bs2_index>]
where N is chain length, eps_Sol is polymer-solvent interaction energy, eps_bssg is binding site-side group interaction energy, and bsi_index is the index of binding site i in the chain

2. Lattice model binding thermodynamics database for heterogeneous chains of different sequences and number of binding sites per chain.

Binding thermodynamics for different sequence defined lattice model chains. The binding sites (BS) interact with binding energy of -6 kBT. The computed thermodynamic quantities are: binding sites binding free energy, dfe, and entropy, tds, and free energy minimum value dfe_min relative to the dissociated state.
