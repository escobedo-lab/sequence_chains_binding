# sequence_chains_binding
Lattice model binding thermodynamics database for heterogeneous chains of different sequences and number of binding sites per chain.

Binding thermodynamics for different sequence defined lattice model chains. The binding sites (BS) interact with binding energy of -6 kBT. The following are the variables explored in the simulations:
1. chain length N
2. binding site-side group interaction energy eps_BSSG
3. sequence of binding sites with 1 indicating a binding site
4. polymer-solvent interaction energy eps_Sol
5. solvent-solvent interaction energy eps_SolSol

The computed thermodynamic quantities are:
1. binding sites binding free energy, dfe, and entropy, tds, relative to the dissociated state
2. free energy minimum value dfe_min
